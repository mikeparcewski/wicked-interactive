How it works

  1. Open your draft in the browser — it renders exactly as it will ship.
  2. Hover to highlight a block, click it, and describe the change you want.
  3. Hit UPDATE — the document regenerates and refreshes itself live.
  4. When you're happy, export a self-contained HTML file or a PDF in one click.
